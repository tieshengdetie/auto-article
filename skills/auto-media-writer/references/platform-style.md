# Platform Style

## Shared Structure

Use this baseline unless the platform section overrides it:

1. Title
2. Opening hook that states the event and why readers care
3. Background and timeline
4. 3-5 second-level headings
5. Analysis of causes, conflict, public emotion, and likely impact
6. Closing viewpoint and interaction question

Generate 3-5 title options for Toutiao, Baijiahao, and Zhihu. Generate 5-8 title options for Xiaohongshu.

## Toutiao

- Use broad-reader language with strong readability.
- Put the newest fact and central conflict in the first 150 characters.
- Prefer concrete titles over vague emotion, e.g. `某事件发酵后，真正让人担心的是这三点`.
- Avoid extreme shock, fear, and guaranteed claims.

## Baijiahao

- Use clearer search-oriented structure.
- Make headings descriptive and fact-forward.
- Add context that helps readers understand background, timeline, and broader meaning.
- Keep the emotional tone restrained but not cold.

## Xiaohongshu

- Use note-like, conversational writing.
- Keep paragraphs short. Use topic tags at the end.
- Emphasize personal feeling, social observation, and practical takeaways.
- Use fewer formal transitions. Avoid stiff phrases such as `综上所述`, `值得注意的是`, and `从某种程度上来说`.
- Use emoji only if the user wants them; otherwise keep clean Chinese punctuation.

## Zhihu

- Start from a question or judgment.
- Explain what happened, why it happened, what people are arguing about, and what conclusion can be drawn.
- Use stronger logic and less emotional push than Toutiao/Xiaohongshu.
- Include uncertainty when sources are incomplete.

## Entertainment

- Length: 500-800 Chinese characters by default. Only expand to 900-1000 characters when the user explicitly asks for deeper analysis or when the source pack contains a verified timeline that needs more context.
- Tone: lively, gossip-aware, skeptical, and readable.
- Focus on timeline, public reaction, contradictions, and what the incident reveals about celebrity ecology.
- Do not assert private facts without reliable sourcing. Use `据公开报道`, `从目前信息看`, or omit uncertain claims.

## Society And General News

- Length: 1500-2500 Chinese characters.
- Tone: hotspot commentary plus emotional resonance.
- Explain why ordinary readers care.
- Avoid empty preaching. Tie the final view back to concrete facts.

## Humanizing Pass

Rewrite after the first draft:

- Replace generic transitions with natural sentence-level connections.
- Cut repetitive summaries and obvious filler.
- Vary paragraph length.
- Add concrete nouns and verbs from the source pack.
- Keep a human editorial stance, but do not add unsupported facts.
- Remove AI tells: `在当今社会`, `不可忽视的是`, `这无疑`, `引发了广泛关注` when repeated, `我们应该理性看待` as a lazy ending.
